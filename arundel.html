Profile

The project treats the garden as living infrastructure — not decoration. Shape is guided by slope and water logic. Planting is layered for seasonal depth and habitat structure.

Form is derived from context: soil, aspect, micro-topography, and remnant ecological pattern. Construction choices are made for longevity and ecological function — permeability, moisture retention, and refuge.

The aim is compounding improvement: baseline, evidence, change over time — with reports that keep the profile honest.
