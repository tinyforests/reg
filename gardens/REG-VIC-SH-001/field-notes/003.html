Why this matters

  • • Paths can either fragment habitat or become low-impact access corridors.
  • • Sub-base decisions affect infiltration, runoff direction, and soil moisture patterns.
  • • Loader use introduces disturbance risk — documenting mitigation protects credibility.

Mitigation & design controls (edit)

  • • Defined travel lines for machinery (minimise compaction footprint).
  • • No geofabric in designated refuge nodes.
  • • Edge zones rehabilitated with litter/mulch + planting.
  • • Water behaviour checked during/after rain.
