Why this matters

  • • Weed pressure reduction releases indigenous structure to establish.
  • • Low disturbance maintains soil aggregates and fungal networks.
  • • Chemical avoidance reduces risk to amphibians and invertebrates.
  • • Litter retention preserves microclimate and moisture buffering.

Immediate response

  • • Prioritise hand removal in refuge zones (avoid compaction).
  • • Retain leaf litter bands where possible.
  • • Spot-mark any recruitment and exclude from disturbance.
