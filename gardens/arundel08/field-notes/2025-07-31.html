What the analysis tells us

  • -- The site sits within what was Grassy Woodland pre-1770 -- the same EVC now forms the basis for species selection across the entire garden.
  • -- Present-day remnant vegetation is highly fragmented. The nearest intact EVCs are confined to small patches near Wattle Park and the Creekline along Riversdale Road.
  • -- The PPRZ zone directly adjoining the property boundary confirms the garden's corridor node potential -- Surrey Hills Reserve is not across the road, it is next door.
  • -- Valley Grassy Forest in the drainage line to the south-east informs the moisture zone planting -- the lower slope of the Arundel garden mimics this condition with swale planting and embedded habitat structure.
