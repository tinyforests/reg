Field Note Standard

Each field note should read like field evidence — what happened, why it matters, what changed, and what proof exists. The goal is clarity and credibility.

Events are single-date and should be used when something materially shifts ecological function, or when verification occurs (rain behaviour, soil tests, species sightings).

Include
  • • What happened
  • • Why it matters
  • • What changed
  • • Evidence (photo/test)
  • • Badge + pillar impacts
  • • Next action / verification step
