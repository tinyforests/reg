Narrow bed along brick wall -- existing plants cleared

The bed runs the length of the brick wall between the paved courtyard and the house. Existing climbing rose with red flowers retained mid-bed. Fresh soil exposed at base. Established shrubs partially removed. Worker visible on ladder in the background -- the site is active across multiple zones simultaneously.

Three zones, one installation window

  • -- The entrance garden (8x1m), scented tunnel pathway (2x4m), and driveway garden (3x3m) are being prepared simultaneously across a 10-day installation window from 17 to 26 November.
  • -- Removing existing plants before new soil amendment and planting ensures the full palette goes into prepared ground -- a cleaner result with no competition from legacy root systems.
  • -- The narrow bed along the brick wall will become the entrance garden -- a calm welcoming edge that frames movement from the street into the home.
