Private Gardens as Ecological Infrastructure

View Example Garden

Most urban gardens contribute little to biodiversity.

The Ecological Registry exists to change that. We establish baseline function, verify improvements, and recognise gardens that move from decoration to ecological contribution.

Explore Registered Garden How It Works

1. Baseline

A garden is assessed across structural diversity, indigenous ratio, soil health, habitat, water function and connectivity.

2. Verification

Dated field notes and photo documentation create a transparent record of ecological function over time.

3. Recognition

Gardens earn badges and measurable scores — creating trust, comparability and long-term ecological value.
