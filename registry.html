Ecological Registry

Registered Gardens

A growing registry of private gardens being re-understood as ecological assets: measurable, mappable, and capable of contributing to urban biodiversity infrastructure.

Registry logic

Gardens can now be recorded, scored, verified, and understood as part of a wider urban ecological network.

Each listing links to a score profile with assessment data, badges, and upgrade opportunities.

This creates a new cultural and technical layer between private stewardship and public ecological value.
